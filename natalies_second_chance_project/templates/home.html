Community Outreach

Community Food Bank

Natalie's Second Chance offers a food bank for pet owners in the community that need assistance feeding their pets. Dog food and sometimes cat food, is available through us at no charge and no requirements for this service.

We encourage anyone needing assistance in feeding their pets to come pick up food during our open adoption hours. Donations of any brand of food (wet or dry) for the food bank are accepted and can be dropped off during our open adoption hours. If you have a food donation to give, but are unable to bring it to us, please contact us to arrange pick up. For the food bank, we also accept open bags of food and treats.

Dog obedience training

If you have a dog that just isn't listening or if you are a new pet owner and don't know what to do, Scott Stone is here to help you. Scott is now offering obedience training at our shelter. He has had extensive training and has worked with a variety of dogs. Follow him on facebook to get all of the current details on classes and services.
